Negroni

A bitter aperitivo from Italy

Ingredients

  • Gin
  • Campari
  • Vermouth
  • Orange

Steps

  1. Add a large ice cube or several smaller ones to an Old Fashioned glass or similar
  2. Take a thin slice of orange or two and add to the glass
  3. Measure equal parts of the alcohol (singles for the sensible, doubles for the brave) and stir through the glass
